The City Temperature Dashboard is a project of Scientists for Future Austria.

Takehome Messages

  • Exposition to heat is a significant threat to public health, especially in urban areas.
  • We measured the temperature at different locations on Siebenbrunnenplatz Vienna.
  • Heat islands can be very local even on the scale of 10 m
  • The results of this project demonstrate that even small adaptation measures, such as a single tree, can make a big difference.
